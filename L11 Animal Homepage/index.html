Overview

In the event of a natural disaster, the Indigenous Animals Rescue Service goes to the front lines to protect and preserve the natural wildlife impacted in the disaster.

Rescue

Efforts

The rescue effort requires able bodied individuals who have desires to help the environment. Any efforts, big or small, are not only adequate, but encouraged. We at the Indigenous Animals Rescue Service firmly believe that every effort is its own reward and that together, we can make a world of difference!

Animal Care

Here at the Indigenous Animals Rescue Service, our goal is to keep the wildlife wild. While our expert animal health specialists take excellent care of the animals, we do what we can to avoid domestication. In doing so, we ensure the best chance for survival when the animals are returned to their homes when it is safe to do so.

Measuring

Success

Success is measured in our best efforts, but also in ensuring that every animal that comes into our care is properly cared for. We aim to be able to care for 100% of all animals brought to our care. We also determine success to be measured by community involvement in our rescue efforts.

Join the Cause

Whether that help is small in scale, the difference could be lifechanging for another. Be the difference you want to see in this world, join the cause today!
